# Problematic Internet Use: Scope Brief

## Working Scope
This swarm studies problematic internet use (PIU) as a psychiatric and behavioral-science topic. The working scope includes broad problematic internet use and specific internet-use disorders when relevant, especially internet gaming disorder (IGD).

## Guardrails
-   Distinguish high engagement from clinically impairing use.
-   Treat screening scores, symptom scales, and formal diagnoses as different levels of evidence.
-   Be explicit when evidence comes from adolescent samples, convenience samples, or gaming-specific cohorts.
